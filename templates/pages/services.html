Our Services

Comprehensive supply chain solutions from global sourcing to last-mile delivery — so you can focus on making great products.

Import Sourcing

We identify and qualify certified suppliers worldwide for food additives, functional ingredients, and raw materials. Every supplier in our network undergoes rigorous vetting to ensure product quality, documentation compliance, and reliable production capacity.

  • Supplier qualification and auditing
  • Certificate of Analysis (CoA) and specification management
  • Regulatory documentation (HALAL, Kosher, food-grade certs)
  • Competitive pricing through direct supplier relationships

Export & Distribution

We manage the full export and regional distribution process, connecting manufacturers in Thailand and Southeast Asia with the ingredients they need. Our logistics network ensures timely, intact delivery across the region.

  • Regional distribution across Southeast Asia
  • Import/export documentation and customs clearance
  • Full and partial container load options
  • Shipment tracking and delivery coordination

Warehousing & Logistics

Our storage facilities are designed for food-grade products, with temperature-controlled environments and strict inventory management. We hold buffer stock of high-demand ingredients so you can order on shorter lead times.

  • Temperature-controlled and dry storage
  • Lot tracking and FIFO inventory management
  • Local stock availability for fast turnaround
  • Repackaging and labelling to your requirements

Technical Support

Our team provides product knowledge and application support to help you select the right ingredient for your formulation. We bridge the gap between global supplier specifications and your production needs.

  • Ingredient selection and application guidance
  • Product specification and regulatory review
  • Sample sourcing and trial order facilitation
  • Ongoing account management and after-sales support
